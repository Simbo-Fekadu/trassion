Integrated Management System (IMS)

Transsion Holdings Ethiopia is certified under ISO 9001, demonstrating our commitment to quality management and continuous improvement.

Our Commitment

We strive to:

  • Provide high-quality products and services that exceed customer expectations.
  • Continuously improve processes and operations for better efficiency.
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.
  • Maintain a culture of excellence, safety, and responsibility.
  • Quality Management: Our IMS ensures that all products and services meet the highest quality standards. Processes are regularly monitored and improved to enhance customer satisfaction, reduce defects, and increase operational efficiency.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We actively minimize our environmental impact through sustainable practices, efficient resource usage, and compliance with environmental regulations, promoting a greener and safer future.
  • Health and Safety: Employee well-being is a priority. Our IMS enforces strict safety protocols, training programs, and risk assessments to ensure a safe and secure workplace.
  • Energy Management: Energy efficiency is integrated into operations to reduce consumption, lower costs, and support sustainable business practices, aligning with ISO 50001 standards.
  • Continuous Improvement: All IMS processes are regularly reviewed and audited. Lessons learned and corrective actions help us innovate, improve performance, and maintain excellence across the organization.

Our Vision: We aim to be a leader in integrating quality, sustainability, safety, and energy efficiency into every aspect of our operations, ensuring long-term value for customers and stakeholders.

Training and Awareness: Regular training programs ensure all employees understand and follow IMS principles, fostering a culture of responsibility and excellence throughout the organization.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Transsion Holdings Ethiopia is officially certified under ISO 9001:2015, reflecting our commitment to Quality Management Systems, operational excellence, and continuous improvement.
